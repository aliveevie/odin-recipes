The shinkafa da wake recipes

This is a traditonal hausa food called "shinkfa da wake", or it's sometimes called "garau garau"

The Ingredients of the food

  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Oil
  • Salads
  • Cucumber
  • Cabbage
  • pepper
  • onion

The steps

  1. Boil water
  2. pour beans
  3. cook the beans for half
  4. Pour the rice
  5. Cut cucumber, cabbage and Salad
  6. finish the cooking first
  7. Put the cut recipes in the cooking
